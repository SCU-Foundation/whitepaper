# SCU Whitepaper v1.7 — Release Notes

**Edition:** v1.7  
**Type:** Architectural Publication  
**Status:** Legacy Stable  
**Publisher:** SCU Foundation

SCU Whitepaper v1.7 represents the final pre‑Ecosystem‑Era edition of the **Superconducting Compute Unit (SCU) architectural definition**.  
It documents the conceptual model, physical structure, and operational characteristics of the SCU hardware architecture as understood at the time of publication.

This edition is preserved for archival completeness and historical reference.

---

## Summary of Changes (Compared to v1.6)

- Refined description of the SCU’s superconducting compute substrate  
- Updated architectural diagrams and component relationships  
- Improved explanation of qubit‑adjacent superconducting pathways  
- Clarified distinctions between logical, physical, and operational layers  
- Removed transitional or experimental architectural sections  
- Strengthened definitions of core SCU primitives and data pathways  
- Consolidated terminology for architectural consistency
